Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 23, 2025

This app respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information the app collects, how it is used, and your choices.

Summary

  • On-device storage: Reflections, journal entries, and insights are stored locally on your device.
  • No tracking: The app does not use advertising networks or track you across apps or websites.
  • No selling: The app does not sell your data.

Information We Collect

User content you create in the app (such as reflections, journal entries, or quiz responses) is stored on your device.

The app does not require an account and does not collect personally identifying information such as your name, email address, phone number, or precise location.

How We Use Information

Your content is used only to provide app features such as journaling, reflections, and generating insights within the app experience.

Data Sharing

We do not share your personal data with third parties.

Analytics & Advertising

The app does not use third-party analytics SDKs, advertising SDKs, or trackers.

Data Retention

Your content remains on your device unless you delete it. If the app includes a “Reset” or delete feature, using it will remove your stored content from the device.

Your Choices

  • You can delete your journal entries and reflections within the app (if available).
  • You can remove the app at any time, which removes locally stored data on that device.

Children’s Privacy

This app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Important Note

This app is intended for self-reflection and personal growth only. It does not provide medical, psychological, or mental health advice and is not a substitute for professional care.
